Ironiya sudby ili s legkim parom!


Directed by: Eldar Ryazanov

Realese date: 1976

Length: 184 min

Category: melodrama, tragicomedy


"Irony of Fate or Enjoy Your Bath!" - Soviet two-part television film directed by Eldar Ryazanov, created in 1975 and first shown in the Soviet Union on January 1, 1976 at 17:45 on the first program of Central Television. The audience of the first show is estimated at 100 million viewers; already on February 7, the film was repeated at the popular request of the audience. Until 1978, according to Fyodor Razzakov, the film "was watched on TV by about 250 million people". The shortened film was also shown in cinemas, and in this form it was watched by about 7 million viewers. In 1977 he was awarded the USSR State Prize.
