The Green Mile


Directed by: Darabont Frank

Realese date: 06.12.1999 (world)

Length: 189 minutes

Category: drama


The green mile is the hallway that a death row inmate walks from his cell to the electric chair. John Coffey, convicted of infanticide, will have to pass it.
